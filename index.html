About Me!
I'm Jonathan Segura, a certified graphic designer, developer, and self-taught creator who doesn't wait for the future I build it, test it, break it, and put it back together until it works as it should... or better. I combine automation, artificial intelligence, and blockchain to create projects that breathe creativity and operate with relentless logic (you know, if it works, don't touch it). Every line of code aims to solve something real. Some say I'm a developer, but in reality, I'm more of a digital alchemist—mixing AI, n8n, bits, and coffee until something life-like emerges from the screen. I move between creative processes and engineering with the same naturalness that a musician improvises a melody: exploring, making mistakes, and fine-tuning. I don't believe in "I already know how to do it." I believe in "what if I try it differently?" That's why I keep learning, experimenting, and creating functional systems. Currently, I focus on projects where code feels like art—and art, like a new way of programming! LinkedIn: @Joalseca Instagram: @Js4art X: @Joalseca GitHub: BitxelRoads
